About Yasaman

Yasaman Jamiyeva is a passionate young artist, blending feminine grace and emotional depth through her drawings and paintings. Her works invite viewers into an intimate world of color, light, and stories.

From participating in prominent resim sergisi (painting exhibitions) to constantly refining her style, Yasaman creates art that resonates timelessly.
